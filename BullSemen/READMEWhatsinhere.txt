What's in BullSemen/

You will find BullSemenMetadata/ that contains:
the manifest file needed for initial qiime comands (QiimeManifestBullSemen/)
and 
the metadata file needed throughout the qiime pipeline (QiimeMetadataBullSemen/)


You will find BullSemenScripts/ that contains:
the co-occurence script used in R studio to perform network and co-occurence analyses (CooccurenceRScriptBullSemen/)
and
the main script used in R studio to perform alpha and beta diversity analyses and obtain taxanomic and differentially abundant genera plots (MainRScriptBullSemen/)
and
the main script/commands used in Qiime2 to analyze sequence quality and obtain preliminary outputs needed for R (MainQiimeScriptBullSemen/)
